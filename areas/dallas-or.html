Serving Dallas & Polk County

Dallas and the broader Polk County area represent an underserved market for quality IT services. Willametro IT bridges that gap — providing the same level of professional low voltage installation, network infrastructure, and managed IT that Salem businesses rely on, now available for Dallas, Monmouth, Independence, and surrounding communities.

Whether you're opening a new business on Main Street, doing a commercial buildout, or simply need your existing network assessed and upgraded, Willametro IT makes the drive to Polk County and brings fully licensed, professional-grade services with us.

We offer free initial consultations for all Dallas and Polk County small businesses.

Common services we provide to Dallas and Polk County businesses include: security camera installation, ethernet and data cable wiring for offices and retail spaces, commercial Wi-Fi design and access point installation, VoIP phone system setup, and ongoing computer and network support. Whether you are opening a new location or upgrading an existing space, we handle the entire project from wiring to configuration.
