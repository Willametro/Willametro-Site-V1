Your local technology partner — based in Salem, built for Salem businesses.

Serving Salem's Small Business Community

Willametro IT Solutions LLC is a Salem-based company, so Salem is home turf. From downtown businesses on Commercial Street to office parks off Commercial SE and retail along Lancaster Drive, we understand the local business landscape and provide technology services built around how Salem companies actually operate.

As an Oregon LE-B licensed contractor, we handle the physical low voltage infrastructure that most IT companies can't — structured cabling, security cameras, access control, and AV systems — and then layer managed IT, networking, and cybersecurity on top. One company, one call, from the wire in the wall to the cloud.

We offer free initial consultations for all Salem small businesses. No commitment, no sales pressure — just an honest look at your current setup and your options.
